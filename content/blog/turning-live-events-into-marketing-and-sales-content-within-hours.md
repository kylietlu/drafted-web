<p data-start="243" data-end="445"><strong data-start="243" data-end="255">Company:</strong> PhotonDelta (in partnership with WeTakeFive)<br data-start="302" data-end="305"><strong data-start="305" data-end="315">Focus:</strong> Event content, real-time publishing, LinkedIn amplification<br data-start="375" data-end="378"><strong data-start="378" data-end="393">Constraint:</strong> Same-day turnaround, live input, limited capacity</p><p data-start="145" data-end="205"><strong>Live content works like news: timing is what gives it value.</strong></p><p data-start="207" data-end="385"><strong>Publishing days later, you&rsquo;re no longer boots-on-the-ground and part of the conversation, you&rsquo;re just summarizing it. The audience might have already moved on, and whatever energy was in the room is fading. That&rsquo;s what makes event content tricky. The value isn&rsquo;t just in what you write, it&rsquo;s in when you publish it.</strong></p><h2 data-section-id="a2je7u" data-start="679" data-end="689">Context</h2><p data-start="691" data-end="849">PhotonDelta needed same-day content coverage during their PIC Summit Europe event, with the goal of publishing and promoting insights while the event was still happening.</p><h2 data-section-id="ii4ghz" data-start="851" data-end="865">My approach</h2><p data-start="867" data-end="1030">I worked closely with the We Take Five team in a lean setup. With the team down a person, we alternated between live note-taking and writing to keep things moving.</p><p data-start="1032" data-end="1196">Across the two-day event, we delivered multiple blogs, including morning recaps and session roundups, all written, published, and promoted on LinkedIn within hours.</p><p data-start="1198" data-end="1440">On day two, capacity was tighter due to filming and SME interviews running in parallel. Instead of stretching output, we focused on a single, comprehensive wrap-up blog that captured the key themes and insights, and published it the same day.</p><h2 data-section-id="1j4wyqd" data-start="1690" data-end="1703">The result</h2><p data-start="1464" data-end="1531">Instead of content trailing behind the event, it became part of it.&nbsp;Insights were shared while they were still relevant, giving attendees something to revisit and giving those not present a way to follow along in real time.</p><p data-start="1800" data-end="1897">Blogs were published during the event, not after, and supported real-time engagement on LinkedIn.</p><p data-start="1899" data-end="2126">Day 1 Morning:&nbsp;<a href="https://www.photondelta.com/news/pse-day-1-morning-wrap-up/" target="_new" rel="noopener" data-start="1916" data-end="1975">https://www.photondelta.com/news/pse-day-1-morning-wrap-up/</a><br data-start="1975" data-end="1978">Day 1 Afternoon:&nbsp;<a href="https://www.photondelta.com/news/pse-day-1-afternoon-recap/" target="_new" rel="noopener" data-start="1997" data-end="2056">https://www.photondelta.com/news/pse-day-1-afternoon-recap/</a><br data-start="2056" data-end="2059">Day 2 Wrap:&nbsp;<a href="https://www.photondelta.com/news/pse-day-2-wrap-up/" target="_new" rel="noopener" data-start="2073" data-end="2124">https://www.photondelta.com/news/pse-day-2-wrap-up/</a></p><p data-start="2128" data-end="2178"><em data-start="2128" data-end="2178">Note: Minor edits may have been made since publishing.</em></p><p><iframe title="YouTube video player" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/oZozv2R8H8w?si=opp0KOxq4ZVcBW53" width="560" height="315" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin"></iframe></p>
