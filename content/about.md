## Meet Drafted.

**Hi, I'm Kylie! The one-woman show behind Drafted.**

I was born and raised in Los Angeles but I've spent nearly half my life in the Netherlands, so I'm pretty much the best of both worlds: combining Dutch directness with American friendliness and a positive, can-do attitude. I'm not afraid to say when something doesn't make sense, and I'm happy to come up with a plan to fix it.

**I'm not _chill_ about great writing, and I don't want to be.**

I've always loved writing and working with content. Somewhere between editing my high school yearbook, friends' CVs, and spotting typos on brochures, billboards, and restaurant menus, I became the founder of a book club that reads the best classic literature from the last hundred years and someone who does the New York Times daily mini puzzles every morning. I'm wired for language. Always have been.

When I'm not working, I'm either reading, lifting weights, or watching reality TV, because sometimes you just need a little brain rot.

## Founding Drafted.

After nearly a decade of working in B2B marketing, I've seen how easily good ideas get lost in processes. Content shouldn't take six weeks of meetings, and campaigns shouldn't stall just because someone's out of office.

Today, I work with a myriad of B2B tech companies across cloud, SaaS, and AI to help teams sharpen their messaging, speed up delivery, and stay sane when things get busy. I also love working with consumer-facing brands looking to connect with their English-speaking audiences. Whether that's a European business expanding internationally or a local company that knows its customers are searching in English, if the tone is right and the brief makes sense, I'm in.
